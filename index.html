Easy Reader

Instruction Manual

Step 1: The Gateway

Click the large central arrow to enter the workspace. This tool is designed to reduce visual stress.

Step 2: Input Content

You can type directly into the central box, paste text, or use a URL link to import school assignments.

Step 3: Visual Adjustments

Use the sidebar to change fonts to OpenDyslexic, adjust line spacing for better tracking, and swap to High Contrast mode to reduce glare.

Step 4: Audio Support

Open the 'Text to Speech' tab to listen to your text. You can adjust the playback speed to suit your listening pace.
